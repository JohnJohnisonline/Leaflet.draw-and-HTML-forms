"I know a spot" Community Scenery Map.

Welcome to the "I know a spot" a community scenery map aimed at showcasing the hidden gems and unique viewpoints known to locals. By adding your favorite scenic spots to the map, you can help visitors and newcomers discover the beauty of our area. To submit a new scenery, use the tools on the upper left pane. After drawing location, a form will pop up prompting more information, fill this out and submit.
